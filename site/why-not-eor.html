Why not EOR

EOR is not a shield when facts show direct control.

Recruiters find people. EORs employ people. We design risk-proof structures that stand up to China labor scrutiny.

China disputes focus on facts.

Titles and contracts are secondary. Control, dependency, and day-to-day management are the key drivers in disputes.

Our work focuses on these facts.

When EOR can still help

EOR can be useful for payroll, local benefits, and quick setup. It is not a substitute for risk control in China if you direct daily work.
