# Recommended Website Style (AI‑Readable)

## Overall Tone
- Professional
- Calm authority
- Compliance‑first
- No hype, no buzzwords

## Target Persona
- SEA tech founder / HR / COO
- Risk‑aware, not law expert
- Wants clarity and control

## Language Rules
- Short sentences
- Business English
- Avoid legal jargon unless necessary
- Explain consequences, not laws

## Visual Style
- Minimalist
- Enterprise SaaS feel
- White / light gray background
- Dark blue or charcoal primary color
- Subtle accent (teal or muted green)

## Layout
- Clear hierarchy
- One primary CTA per page
- Generous whitespace
- No stock photos of people smiling

## Trust Signals
- Process diagrams
- Comparison tables
- Clear deliverables
- Calm confidence

## What to Avoid
- Founder stories
- Awards with no relevance
- Buzzwords like “disruptive”, “innovative”
- Over‑designed animations
