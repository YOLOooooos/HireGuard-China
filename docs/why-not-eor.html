Why not EOR

This is not EOR and not recruitment.

Recruiters focus on who to hire. EORs focus on who employs the person. We focus on how a role should exist so your business does not stop when a person changes.

Where EOR makes sense

If you want payroll outsourcing, EOR can help. If you want long-term control and flexibility, it will not solve your problem.
